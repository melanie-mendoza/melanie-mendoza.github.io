Why Hire Me?

“My job as a registered nurse for 15 years taught me the values of critical thinking, empathy, and collaboration. I knew early on that I had a knack for understanding people from different perspectives, but I was also passionate about improving systems and delivering quality. As medicine continually advances with the use of technology, I found myself drawn to software engineering. I wanted to make a difference in the quality of people's lives, but most importantly, I wanted to show my son that he can and should find joy in what he does. By applying my core principles to all the projects I've been a part of so far, I’ve helped exceed company goals. In the future, I’m excited to work for a company that puts their customers first and delivers on their promises of quality and excellence.”
